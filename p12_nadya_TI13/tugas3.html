Atomic Habits
The book Atomic Habits is a book about how to change our habits for the better and of course become permanent changes. James Clear teaches us to achieve our long term goals through small habits that we do every day.

Think and Grow Rich
Think and Grow Rich (1937) is a classic book containing recipes for success adapted from the experiences of more than 500 people in America who have succeeded in achieving abundant wealth despite starting their lives from "zero point".
